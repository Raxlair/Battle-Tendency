Demon Slayer

Damn, this show is some of the worst shit I have ever seen, even though I have only seen the first two seasons and the Mugen Train movie. To be fair the animation is splendid but I hate everything else about it To start off, I hate all characters over than Rengoku. Especially Zenitsu. He is the most annoying, dumb and creepy character I have ever seen. But only slightly better than this we have the MC Tanjiro, who wants to save the Demons even though they killed all of his family. If I were Tanjiro I would try to kill all the Demons, which shows how unrealistic the show is.
2/10
